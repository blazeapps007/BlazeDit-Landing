The Uprising

What we're fighting for

⚠ They built a cage and called it a platform

Every major social platform runs the same racket: you supply the content, they hold the keys. One policy memo, one advertiser tantrum, one opaque algorithmic purge — and years of your voice, your community, your audience disappear without warning or appeal.

And it's not accidental. The addiction is the product. Your rage, your anxiety, your compulsive scrolling — that's their revenue model.

🌐 We tore the walls down

BlazeDit runs on a decentralized protocol — your posts live on infrastructure owned by no single company. There is no "BlazeDit server" to raid, no central admin to bribe, no board of directors to threaten into compliance. The network was born free. It stays free.
